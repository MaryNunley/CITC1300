Suspenseful

A Perfect Blood is the tenth book in the The Hollows series by Kim Harrison, set in a supernatural world filled with witches, vampires, demons, and various other paranormal beings. The story follows Rachel Morgan, a witch and bounty hunter, who is struggling with her own personal issues and the complexities of living in a world where humans and supernatural creatures coexist. Rachel is trying to regain control over her life, particularly after the events in the previous book where her powers were altered and her relationships tested

Summary

In this book, Rachel is called to investigate a series of murders where the victims have been drained of blood in a manner that suggests the work of a vampire. However, she soon learns that there is something more sinister going on. The blood is not simply being taken; it is being used for a dark ritual that could have disastrous consequences for the world. Rachel teams up with her friends, including Ivy, her vampire partner, and Jenks, a pixy, to uncover the truth behind these killings and stop a dangerous plot.
