How to Play!

Things you will need:

  • A deck of 52 cards (Bicycle)
  • A Blackjack mat
  • 2 or more players
  • Chips for betting (optional)

Rules

The general idea is to beat the dealers hand with cards valued at 21 or as close as you can get to this number without going over. This can be played with 2 or more people. Each player will start with 2 cards, both face up. The dealer will have one card face down and the other face up. After the cards are dealt the players decide if they want to HIT (take another card to increase their value),or STAND(keep your current card value and not take any more cards from the dealer).

Card Values

Number cards (2-10) are worth their face value. A 2 is worth 2 points, a 7 is worth 7 points, etc. Kings, Queens, and Jacks are also known as face cards. These are worth 10 points each. Aces are worth either 1 or 11 points. This depends on which value would be best for the player. For example, if the player is dealt an Ace and a 2, it would be best for the player to value the Ace at 11 so the total value is now 13. When the player hits and a Jack is dealt to the player the value would be 23 which is called a BUST. This means the player went over the set value of 21 and lost. However, if the player sets the Ace value to 1, the new total will be 13 once again and the player has another chance to HIT and get 21.
